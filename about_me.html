About Me

Recent graduate from the University of Illinois. I love reading books, watching movies, cooking, dancing, meeting new people and traveling. The qualities that define me are I am a deligent worker, my zeal to keep learning and keep outperforming myself, my dedication towards my work and my team. My Weakness however is that at times I am "too direct" with people. Sometimes I can be a bit too honest when I provide feedback to coworkers. My personality is naturally very straightforward and to the point, and most of my colleagues really value that, but I have learned that there are times on the job when more diplomacy is required. Some people would consider the fact that I have never worked in this field before as a weakness. However, being highly trainable and open minded, I have no pre-conceived notions on how to perform my job. Working with your organization will give me the opportunity to learn the job the way you want it done, not the way I believe it is done. In addition, although I have no former on-the-job experience, I do bring with me extensive hands-on training and experience which can only enhance my ability to learn extremely quickly.
